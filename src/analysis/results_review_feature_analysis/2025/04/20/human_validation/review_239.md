## レビュー本文
### 原文
If you are looking for a small, portable basic AM/FM radio, you can't beat this. It has regular dial tuning, a fold out antenna, strap and most importantly clear reception on AM and FM channels. Nothing fancy at all about this little radio, but it works great and is definitely a great price!
